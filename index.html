Environmental Policies

Change Makers

Our political party's views align predominantly with a left-wing perspective when it comes to environmental policies. Our core values include a commitment to preserving the environment by encouraging sustainable practices and advocating for the conservation and protection of natural resources. We believe in supporting initiatives aimed to promote renewable energy and eco-friendly practices within communities and cities. By prioritizing these environmental principles, our party's position falls within the left end of the political spectrum when it comes to this issue.
